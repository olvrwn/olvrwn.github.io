FitnessBase

About This Project

FitnessBase is an ad free and in-app-purchases free fitness application. It was developed by me, Oliver Dieke, with support for the informational part from Tom Niklas Nestler.


Features:

- Start a training in one of three difficulties

- The app keeps track of how you're performing and adjusts itself automatically

- And of course: no data is stored, collected, processed or evaluated on servers
